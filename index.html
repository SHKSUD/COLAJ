The Man Behind the Mission

Soldier. Scholar.
Storyteller.

Born in 1969 in Jammu to a family that migrated from Pakistan-Occupied J&K during the 1947 partition, Colonel Ajay K Raina was commissioned into the Indian Army in 1990. He proudly commanded his parent unit, 93 Field Regiment, and served with Rashtriya Rifles in North Kashmir — earning the Sena Medal for Gallantry.

After 27 years of distinguished service, he took premature retirement in 2017 to pursue his passion for military history, nation-building, and truth-telling through writing and speaking. Today he stands as one of India's most prolific voices on defence, Kashmir, and national heritage.
